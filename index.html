Jalisco, Mexico

Jalisco is a state found in southwestern Mexico. At it's center is Mexico's second largest city Guadalajara. This city is where mariachi originated and is well known for it's production of tequila. You can also find beautiful beaches to the far west of the state inluding, Puerto Vallarta, home to many tourists.
